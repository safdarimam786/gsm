G.S.M. Sr. SECONDARY SCHOOL.

B Block Lohia Nagar, Ghaziabad was established in 1992 by Geeta Sanjay Memorial Public Educational Society in a pollution-free area in the heart of Ghaziabad.

The school is English medium and purely co-educational. It is affiliated to CBSE (10+2) New Delhi. The school is providing Science (PCM, PCB), Commerce and Humanities up to Senior Secondary level. The school campus has about two acres of land, triple storied building with a large playground and gardening. School has one national level kho-kho playground. The studies are through Smart classes. There are more then 25 cameras inside the classes and campus for 24*7 surveillance.

The pattern of education is based on the real needs of every child with a view to promote his/her intelligence, grasping power, reasoning abilities and application or acquired knowledge. It also ensures that every child develops the qualities of trust, tolerance, integrity, love and compassion to become a good citizen of India.
